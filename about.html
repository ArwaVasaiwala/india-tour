About IndiaTour

We are passionate about showcasing the beauty and diversity of India to travellers across the world.

Our Story

IndiaTour was founded with a simple mission — make it easy for every traveller to discover the incredible destinations India has to offer. From the snowy peaks of the Himalayas to the sunny shores of Goa, we curate the best experiences for you.

Our platform brings together verified place listings, authentic user reviews, and an AI-powered travel assistant to help you plan the perfect trip — whether you're a solo backpacker or a family on vacation.
